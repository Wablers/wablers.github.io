TOEFL Vocabulary - List 6

Author: Wablers Date: June 1, 2024  13:23:39 Category: TOEFL

V6-1

1. Rhinoceros n.

犀牛

  • Derivative
    • Rhino (keratin) 犀牛角蛋白
  • Expansion
    • Sugar daddy = Dirty old man

2. Herald vt.

  • Synonym
    • Foreshadow 预示
    • Announce 宣告
  • Derivative
    • Heralder 传令官

3. Affirm v.

断言；证实

  • Synonym
    • Assert 坚持声称

  • Phrase
    • Affirmative action 反歧视行动
    • Self-affirmation 自我肯定 != Self-denial 自我否认
  • Roots and Affixes
    • -firm 坚定的

4. Modem n.

调制解调器

  • Expansion
    • Router 路由器

5. Compensate vt.

  • Derivative
    • Compensation 补偿；报酬
  • Synonym
    • Make up for 弥补
    • Remedy n. 补偿；改进；治疗
    • Amend vt. 纠正；改进
  • Expansion
    • It is never too late to mend. 亡羊补牢，为时不晚

6. Wax v./n.

  • Phrase
    • Wax the car 给车打蜡

  • Derivative
    • Psychoanalyst 心理分析学者
