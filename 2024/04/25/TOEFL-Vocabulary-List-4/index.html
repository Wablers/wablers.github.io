TOEFL Vocabulary - List 4

Author: Wablers Date: April 25, 2024  22:14:34 Category: TOEFL

V5-1

1. Envelop vt.

  • Synonym
    • Surround
    • Enclose 闭合
      • Enclosement Movement 圈地运动
  • Phrase
    • Push your envelop = Push your boundary 挑战极限
  • Example
    • Shanghai was envelop by smog. 上海被雾霾笼罩。

2. Storage n.

  • Phrase
    • Storage battery
  • Derivative
    • Store

3. Proactive adj.

积极主动的、先发制人的

  • Defin: Acting in anticipation of future problems, needs or changes.
  • Phrase
    • Take proactive measure to do… 采取积极主动的措施做…

4. Fantasy n.

  • Derivative
    • Fantasize 幻想
    • Fantastical Animals 神奇的动物

5. Posit vt.

  • Synonym
    • Arrange 安排
    • Assume 假定
  • Roots and Affixes
    • -pos 放

6. Disguise vt./n.

掩饰、伪装

  • Phrase
    • A demon in the guise of an angle
    • An angle in disguise

7. Psychology n.

  • Roots and Affixes
    • -psych 心理
      • Psychopath 精神变态者
      • Psychologist 心理医生
      • Psychotherapist 心理疗法
      • Psychiatrist 精神病专家

8. Embellish vt.

添枝加叶

  • Synonym

    • Adorn 装饰

  • Roots and Affixes

    • -em 使动
    • -bell 战争；美女
      • The belle of the ball 舞会的美女

9. Weird adj.

  • Derivative
    • Weirdo 怪人
    • Weird-looking man 长相怪异的男人

10. Diverge vi.

（道路、线条）分开、岔开；（意见）分歧

  • Roots and Affixes

    • -di 二
    • -ver 转

  • Example

    • Two roads diverged in the wood.
    • Our paths of live diverged after thar event…
