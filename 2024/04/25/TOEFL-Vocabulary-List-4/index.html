TOEFL Vocabulary - List 4

Author: Wablers Date: April 25, 2024  22:14:34 Category: TOEFL

V5-1

1. Envelop vt.

  • Synonym
    • Surround
    • Enclose 闭合
      • Enclosement Movement 圈地运动
  • Phrase
    • Push your envelop = Push your boundary 挑战极限
  • Example
    • Shanghai was envelop by smog. 上海被雾霾笼罩。

2. Storage n.

  • Phrase
    • Storage battery
  • Derivative
    • Store

3. Proactive adj.

积极主动的、先发制人的

  • Defin: Acting in anticipation of future problems, needs or changes.
  • Phrase
    • Take proactive measure to do… 采取积极主动的措施做…

4. Fantasy n.

  • Derivative
    • Fantasize 幻想
    • Fantastical Animals 神奇的动物

5. Posit vt.

  • Synonym
    • Arrange 安排
    • Assume 假定
  • Roots and Affixes
    • -pos 放

6. Disguise vt./n.

掩饰、伪装

  • Phrase
    • A demon in the guise of an angle
    • An angle in disguise

7. Psychology n.

  • Roots and Affixes
    • -psych 心理
      • Psychopath 精神变态者
      • Psychologist 心理医生
      • Psychotherapist 心理疗法
      • Psychiatrist 精神病专家

8. Embellish vt.

添枝加叶

  • Synonym

    • Adorn 装饰

  • Roots and Affixes

    • -em 使动
    • -bell 战争；美女
      • The belle of the ball 舞会的美女

9. Weird adj.

  • Derivative
    • Weirdo 怪人
    • Weird-looking man 长相怪异的男人

10. Diverge vi.

（道路、线条）分开、岔开；（意见）分歧

  • Roots and Affixes

    • -di 二
    • -ver 转

  • Example

    • Two roads diverged in the wood.
    • Our paths of live diverged after thar event…

11. Infuriate vt.

激怒

  • Derivative
    • Fury = Outrage = Anger 愤怒
    • Furious
  • Example
    • Fast and Furious

12. Spear v./n.

  • Derivative

    • Spearhead v. 矛头

  • Example

  • Charles Darwin spearheaded the study of evolution. 查尔斯·达尔文是进化论研究的先驱

13. Repress vt.

  • Roots and Affixes
    • -press 压
  • Example
    • He felt repressed after his divorce.
    • The police cracked down on/repressed the riot. 警察镇压了骚乱

14. Conquer vt.

  • Example
    • Conquer yourself and the world lies at your feet. 征服自己，世界就在你脚下
    • I came, I saw, I conquered. 我来了，我看见了，我征服了
  • Expansion
    • Caesar
    • Alexander the Great
    • William the Conqueror
    • Genghis Khan

15. Thrust v./n.

  • Synonym
    • Push with a force
    • Shove 推

16. Casualty n.

  • Example
    • Casualty is huge. 伤亡是巨大的

17. Incidental adj.

  • Derivative
    • Incident
      • Five Incidents of bird flu 五起禽流感事件
  • Expansion
    • Accident

18. Superb adj.

极好的、高质量的、华丽的

  • Phrase
    • Superb performance

19. Bestow vt.

  • Synonym

    • Confer 授予

  • Roots and Affixes

    • -fer 带来

  • Example

    • Bestowed by the Queen the knight-ship 被女王授予爵士爵位

20. Adventitious adj.

偶然的、偶发的

21. Resolute adj.

坚决的、果断的

  • Synonym
    • Determined
    • Decisive
  • Example
    • New Year’s Resolution 新年决心

22. Dissipate v.

消散、挥霍

  • Synonym

    • Spread thin 摊薄
    • Scatter 散开

  • Example

    • His passion dissipated…

23. Cranial n.

头盖骨的、颅骨的

  • Phrase
    • Cranial surgery

24. Lineage n.

  • Phrase
    • Matrilineal 母系
    • Patrilineal 父系
  • Roots and Affixes
    • -line
  • Expansion
    • Linear Algebra 线性代数

25. Balk n./v.

障碍、畏缩

  • Synonym

    • Obstacle
    • Road block

  • Example

    • I balked at sight of that tiger. 我一看到那只老虎就退缩了

26. Irritate vt.

激怒、刺激

  • Derivative
    • Irritative 刺激性的
    • Irritating 令人恼火

27. Consolidate v.

巩固、使合并

  • Roots and Affixes
    • -solid
  • Example
    • Alibaba just consolidate a small company. 阿里巴巴只是整合了一家小公司

28. Intervening adj.

介于中间的、发生于期间的

  • Roots and Affixes

    • -inter
    • -ven 来

  • Phrase

    • Foreign intervention 外国干预
    • Intervening time between A and B

29. Corrosion n.

腐蚀

  • Derivative
    • Corrosive
  • Phrase
    • Soil corrosion 土壤腐蚀

30. Mental adj.

  • Phrase
    • Mental health

31. Plump adj.

微胖的、丰满的

  • Synonym
    • Curvy
    • Well-developed

32. Readily adv.

欣然地、容易地

  • Phrase
    • Readily available 一应俱全

33. Cooperative adj.

  • Roots and Affixes
    • -co
      • co-worker
      • Collaborate 合作
  • Expansion
    • Cooperation VS. Competition
    • COO: Chief Operation Officer

34. Pave v.

  • Phrase
    • Pave the way for 为…铺路
  • Derivative
    • Pavements

35. Contamination n.

污染、玷污

  • Example
    • The water is contaminated.

36. Shrink v.

  • Phrase
    • Shake off/from responsibility 逃避责任
    • Clothes shrunk after washed
    • Psychiatrist = shrink （美式替代写法）

37. Pretentious adj.

自负的

  • Derivative
    • Pretend v. 假装
    • Pretention n.
  • Roots and Affixes
    • -pre
    • -tend (tent)
      • Protect
  • Example
    • Your smile looks pretentious. 你的笑容看起来很做作

38. Laser n.

  • Derivative
    • Lasersaber 激光剑
  • Example
    • Saber-toothed cat

39. Indentity n.

40. Barter n./v.

实物交易、以物易物

  • Synonym
    • Trade
    • Deal

41. Cue n.

  • Synonym
    • Hint
    • Clue
  • Example
    • He is clueless about sth… 他对某事一无所知……

42. Tenant n.

房客、承租人

  • Antonym
    • Landlord 店主

43. Medal n.

  • Phrase
    • Gold medal 金牌
  • Derivative
    • Medalist

44. Inscribe v.

写、铭刻

  • Roots and Affixes
    • -scribe 写
      • Describe
      • Prescribe 规定、处方
  • Phrase
    • Inscription on the tablet

45. Flake n./v.

雪花、糖霜、薄片

46. Concert n.

  • Phrase
    • In Concert 齐心协力
    • By concerted effort 通过共同努力
  • Roots and Affixes
    • -cert 心

47. Supreme adj.

  • Phrase
    • Supreme being 至高无上的存在
    • Supreme Court Justices 大法官
  • Derivative
    • Supremacist 至上主义

48. Encounter vt.

  • Synonym
    • Come Across
  • Example
    • When you are encountered/confronted with sth…

49. Lease n./vt.

  • Example
    • It is a lease. 这是租约

50. Downward adv.

  • Roots and Affixes
    • -ward 向
      • Westward
      • Eastward
      • Upward
      • Leftward
  • Phrase
    • Downward spiral 下降通道

51. Courteous adj.

谦恭的、有礼貌的

  • Derivative
    • Courtesy 礼貌
    • Court v. 求爱

52. Evolve v.

  • Roots and Affixes
    • -volv 转、滚
    • -e 出
  • Phrase
    • Evolutionary Tree

53. Jeopardize vt.

破坏、危及

  • Phrase
    • Put sb in jeopardy 将某人置于危险之中

54. Detergent adj./n.

清洁的、净化的

  • Derivative
    • Deter 威慑、阻止
    • Deterrent
  • Example
    • Does death penalty have deterrent effect? 死刑有威慑作用吗

55. Parking

56. Imprint v./n.

使铭记；印记

  • Synonym
    • Engrave
    • Impress
    • Inscribe

57. Degree n.

  • Expansion
    • C = (F - 32) * 5 / 9

58. Bulk n.

  • Example
    • The bulk of our time is spent on… 我们的大部分时间都花在…

59. Spontaneity n.

自发性、自发行为

  • Derivative
    • Spontaneous 自发的

60. Mast n.

船桅

61. Inanimate adj.

  • Antonym

    • Animate 有生气的、有生命的

  • Derivative

    • Animation 动画片
    • Anime 日本动漫

  • Roots and Affixes

    • -anim 生命

62. Exhale v.

呼气

  • Antonym

    • Inhale 吸气

  • Roots and Affixes

    • -ex

63. Comply vi.

  • -

  • Phrase

    • Comply with… 服从

  • Derivative

    • Compliant 顺从的 — Rebellious 叛逆的

  • Roots and Affixes

    • -ply 折叠

64. Plantation n.

种植园

  • Phrase
    • Plantation life

65. Request n./vt.

  • Phrase
    • do sth at sb’s request
  • Roots and Affixes
    • -ques 问
      • Conquest 征服
      • Question

66. Penetrate v.

刺穿、渗透

  • Phrase
    • Penetrate the audience
  • Derivative
    • Penetration
  • Expansion
    • Gene — Meme 文化基因

67. Diversion n.

消遣、转移

  • Roots and Affixes
    • -ver 转

68. Endure vt.

  • Derivative
    • Endurance 耐力、忍受力
  • Roots and Affixes
    • -en
