TOEFL Vocabulary - List 3

Author: Wablers Date: April 22, 2024  21:04:16 Category: TOEFL

V4-1

1. Insulin n.

胰岛素

  • Roots and Affixes
    • -insul 隔离
      • Insulate v.
        • Insulate sb from electric current 将sb与电流隔离
      • -in
        • Biotin 生物素
        • Penicillin 抗生素

2. Explore v.

  • Roots and Affixes
    • -ex 向外
    • -plor 哭求
      • Implore 恳求
  • Phrase
    • Space exploration

3. Cart n./vt.

  • Phrase
    • Golf cart 高尔夫车
    • Put horses behind the card 本末倒置

4. Pertinent adj.

  • Synonym
    • Relevant 相关的
  • Roots and Affixes
    • -per 贯穿始终
    • -tin (-tain) 拿住
  • Expansion
    • Irrevevant 不相关的
      • i + 一个辅音 表否定

5. Mercy n.

  • Phrase
    • Mercy killing 安乐死 = Euthanasia
    • Show me the mercy
    • at the mercy of sb 受sb的摆布
  • Derivative
    • Merciful
    • Merciless

6. Rebellious adj.

  • Derivative
    • Rebel v./n. (动名词相同的单词，动词中音向后一个音节)
  • Roots and Affixes
    • -bell 战争
      • bellicose 好战的
  • Example
    • I rebel against my boss.

7. Barrel n.

  • Phrase
    • A barrel of crude oil 一桶原油
    • A barrel of beer 一桶啤酒
    • Gun barrel 枪管

8. Transition n./v.

  • Phrase

    • v. Transition from A to B 从A过渡到B
    • n. Transition period 过渡期

  • Roots and Affixes

    • -trans 变
      • Transportation 运输
    • -port 运输
      • Port 港口
      • Potter 搬运工

9. Lyric adj./n.

  • Derivative
    • Lyrics 歌词
    • Lyre 里拉乐器
  • Example
    • You feel lyrical today. 你今天感觉很抒情。

10. Adorn vt.

  • Synonym
    • Decorate
  • Derivative
    • Adornment

11. Questionnaire n,

  • Expansion
    • Billonaire
    • Millionaire

12. Minority n.

  • Antonmy

    • Majority

  • Phrase

    • Ethnic minority 少数民族

  • Exapnsion

    • Minor 小的
      • Minors 未成年人

13. Entitle vt.

  • Example
    • Obamacare 奥巴马医改 -> Entitlement 权利 program
    • Everyone is entitled to his own opinions, but not his facts. 每个人都有权发表自己的意见，但无权发表自己的事实。

14. Renew vt.

  • Derivative

    • Renewal n.更新
    • Renewable energy 可再生能源

  • Example

    • I want to renew this book. 续借书本
    • Renew our promise to this nation… 重续我们对这个国家的承诺……

15. Consistent adj.

  • Derivative

    • Consistency 一致，稳定，始终如一

  • Antonym

    • Flip-flop 出尔反尔; 人字拖

  • Roots and Affixes

    • -sist (sit)
    • -con

16. Quotation n.

  • Phrase
    • Famous quote 著名引用
    • Quotation for sth. 某物报价

17. So-called adj.

所谓的

  • Example
    • How have those so-called improvements helped the local community? 这些所谓的改进是如何帮助当地社区的？

18. Magnitude n.

重要度、亮度

  • Roots and Affixes
    • -mag 大
      • Magnifying glass 放大镜
    • -tude …度

19. Rectangle n.

  • Roots and Affixes
    • -rect 直
      • Correct 正确的
      • Rectum 直肠
      • Rectify 修正
      • Resurrect 复活
  • Expansion
    • Angle
    • Square
    • Sphere
    • Ciclre
    • Cube

20. Telescope n.

  • Roots and Affixes
    • -tele 远
      • Telepathy 心有灵犀
    • -path 情感

21. Lightning n.

  • Expansion
    • Thunder < Lightning

22. Utensil n.

用具

  • Roots and Affixes
    • -ut (use) 用
      • Utilize v. 利用
      • Utility n. 效用
        • Utility diminishing 效用减少

23. Kennel n.

狗舍

24. Liquid adj./n.

  • Phrase

    • Rice liquor 米酒

  • Expansion

    • Solid n./adj. 固体
    • Gas -> Gaseous 气体的

25. Remark n.

  • Synonym
    • Comment

26. Principal n./adj.

  • Synonym
    • Major
    • Main
    • Primary
    • President
  • Expansion
    • Principle n. 原则
      • A man of principle 一个有原则的人

27. Hide v./n.

  • Phrase
    • Hide and seek 捉迷藏
    • Animal hdie 动物兽皮

28. Spoil v.

  • Phrase
    • Spoiled child 被宠溺的小孩
    • Spoil the camp 破坏营地
  • Derivative
    • Spoils 战利品

29. Internship n.

实习期

  • Example
    • I work as an intern in the White House. 在白宫实习
  • Expansion
    • Friendship
    • Relationship
    • Sportsmanship 运动员精神

30. Polish v.

31. Starch n.

淀粉

  • Phrase
    • Starchy lawyer 古板的律师

32. Assimilation n.

  • Roots and Affixes
    • a + 辅音 表强调
    • -simil 相同
      • Similar 相似的
  • Phrase
    • Cultural assimilation 文化同化

33. Detest n.

  • Synonym
    • Hate
  • Derivative
    • Detestable 令人厌恶的

34. Gigantic adj.

  • Synonym
    • Huge
    • Enormous

35. Convention n.

  • Phrase
    • Conventional wisdom 传统智慧
    • Party Convention 党大会
    • 公约

36. Diagonal adj./n.

对角线；斜线的

  • Phrase

    • Diagonal Length

  • Roots and Affixes

    • -dia 直径
      • Diameter = 2 * radius 直径 = 2 * 半径
    • -gon 角
      • Pentagon 五角形
      • Hexagon 六角形

37. Absenteeism n.

旷课；旷工

  • Phrase

    • Absense from school 缺课

  • Expansion

    • Play hooky 逃学 = Play truant

38. Bacon n.

39. Silversmith n.

银匠；银器商人

  • Expansion
    • Blacksmith 铁匠
    • Locksmith 锁匠

40. Cell n.

  • Phrase
    • Jail cell 监狱牢房
    • Cellphone 手机

41. Digression n.

跑题

  • Roots and Affixes
    • -di 离开
    • -gress 走

42. Stake n.

  • Synonym
    • Post, Pole, Stick, Spike 柱子，棒子，尖峰
    • Bet 打赌
  • Derivative
    • Stakeholder 利益攸关方
    • Shareholder 股东
  • Example
    • When the stake is high 当赌注很高的时候

43. Pesticide n.

44. Conflict vi/n.

  • Phrase

    • A in conflict with B A 与 B 有冲突

  • Roots and Affixes

    • -con 全，共同

    • -flict 打，击

      • Inflict pain on sb 给sb带来痛苦

45. Accumulate vt.

  • Roots and Affixes
    • -cumul 堆积
      • Cumulus 积云 ☁️
  • Derivative
    • Accumulation

46. Eternal adj.

  • Roots and Affixes
    • -e 外
    • tern (term) 边界
  • Derivative
    • Eternaltity 永恒

47. Sting v.

  • Phrase
    • Sting sb into action 激励某人采取行动

48. Extinct adj.

  • Derivative
    • Extinction 灭绝
    • Mass Extinction

49. Respond vi.

  • Derivative
    • Response

50. Sunset
